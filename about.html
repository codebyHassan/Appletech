Our Story

About Apple Tech

We're a team of technicians who love taking things apart and making them work again.

Precision Micro-Soldering

Where others see a dead logic board, we see a puzzle waiting to be solved under the microscope.

Our Mission

We believe in fixing things instead of throwing them away.

Throwing away a laptop just because a single chip failed never made sense to us. We started this shop to offer real, component-level repairs, and eventually opened the institute to pass those skills on to the next generation of techs.

Our Journey

Company Evolution

2013

The Beginning

Started out fixing friends' iPhones on a kitchen table. Eventually rented a small shop space in the city.

2016

Micro-Soldering Pioneer

Bought our first serious microscope and hot air station. Started offering board-level repairs when most shops were just swapping parts.

2018

Institute Founded

Realized there was a huge shortage of good techs. We opened the Institute to start teaching others how to actually fix logic boards.

2021

Scaling Up

Crossed the milestone of 10,000 successful device repairs. Expanded to a larger facility with dedicated training labs.

2024

Industry Leader

Moved into our current facility, giving us enough room for the main repair lab and a dedicated classroom for our students.

What We Stand For

Our Values

Precision

We try to get every tiny screw back exactly where it belongs.

Integrity

We'll tell you straight up if a repair isn't worth it. No point in fixing something that's totally dead.

Innovation

Apple keeps changing how they build things, so we keep buying new tools and learning new tricks.

Education

We don't gatekeep our repair methods. If we figure out a tough repair, we teach our students how to do it.

Sustainability

Keeping electronics out of landfills is just a good thing to do. Repairing is almost always better than replacing.

Excellence

We take pride in our work. It feels good to hand back a perfectly working laptop to someone who thought it was dead.
